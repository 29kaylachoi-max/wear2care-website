🌿 Our Story

        
            Wear2Care began as a student-led volunteer project that turns old T-shirts into eco-friendly bags. Our goal is simple — to protect the planet and share kindness.

            The name ‘ClosticBags’ comes from ‘Cloth + Plastic Bags’, representing our idea of replacing disposable plastic bags in everyday life. Every bag is handmade with care, and all profits are donated to support those in need.

            “Small actions lead to big change. Let’s shout out Wear2Care!”
